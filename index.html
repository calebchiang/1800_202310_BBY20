Making sidewalks safer for everyone

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly platform that enables individuals to report hazards on sidewalks caused by weather events, and view them on a map in real-time. Our goal is to empower communities to take action and improve safety, particularly in areas affected by the increasingly frequent and severe weather events attributed to global warming. We believe that by working together and making use of technology, we can create a safer, more accessible, and sustainable environment for all.

About us

We are three BCIT Computer Science Technology students in term one and we set out to address some extreme weather problems in some form or another. We have built this web application in hopes that it can help anyone and everyone that relies on a safe environment when they walk while commuting or for leisure.
